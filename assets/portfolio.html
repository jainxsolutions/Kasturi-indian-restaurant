Our Story · 02
— Our Story

Carried from Lucknow
to the heart of Saigon.

Kasturi Indian Restaurant is a warm, welcoming address for Indian families, friends and travellers — a place that tastes like home, served with the kind of care guests remember.

We cook the classics the way they should taste — with generous portions, honest spice, and the warmth of an Indian home. Our recipes are carried straight from the kitchens of Lucknow: slow-cooked curries, tandoor breads pulled hot from the oven, biryanis sealed and opened tableside.

Today we run four restaurants across Vietnam — three in Ho Chi Minh City and one on Phu Quoc — built to host everything from intimate family dinners to full coach groups of two hundred and forty.

— From Our Kitchen

Signature dishes.

N° 01 · Best Seller— Lucknow
Hyderabadi Mutton Biryani

N° 02 · Best Seller— Punjab
Authentic Butter Chicken
Tender chicken cooked in a rich, creamy tomato butter sauce — built from a base of charred tandoori chicken and a long-cooked makhani gravy.
